Are unwatermarked images safe on here?

No, any image posted without a watermark (or hell, with one in some case) is not safe on the internet ever.

I've disabled right clicking on images but there's a bunch of ways to get past this - the best thing for you to do is either make your own thumbnails or slap a big watermark on the images. Or both.
